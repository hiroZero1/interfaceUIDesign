Mejiro Station is a JR Yamanote Line station located in Toshima-ku, Tokyo, situated in a quiet residential area.
Nearby, you'll find Mejiro Gakuen and Gakushuin University, making it a hub for many students.
The beautiful Mejiro Garden is also close by.
The area is dotted with small shopping streets and cafes, perfect for shopping and relaxation.
The station offers convenient transportation, providing easy access to major locations in Tokyo.

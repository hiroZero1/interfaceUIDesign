SHIMURA

Shimura, a beloved sweets shop in Mejiro, Tokyo, is famous for its traditional Japanese desserts.
The shop is best known for its kakigori, a refreshing shaved ice treat, available in various flavors.
Shimura also offers a variety of other classic Japanese sweets, all crafted with care and precision.
The cozy atmosphere and friendly service make it a popular spot for locals and visitors alike.
Shimura is a must-visit for anyone looking to enjoy authentic and delicious Japanese confections.

GELATO_MINNA

GELATO_MINNA, a charming sweets shop in Mejiro, Tokyo, specializes in artisanal gelato.
The shop offers a diverse selection of flavors, made with fresh and high-quality ingredients.
Their unique and seasonal options provide a delightful experience for every visit.
The friendly atmosphere and welcoming staff enhance the enjoyment of their delicious gelato.
GELATO_MINNA is a perfect destination for those looking to savor refreshing and flavorful treats.

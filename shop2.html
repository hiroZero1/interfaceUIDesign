AIGRE DOUCE

AIGRE DOUCE, a delightful patisserie in Mejiro, Tokyo, is renowned for its exquisite cakes and pastries.
The bakery offers a wide variety of sweets, including seasonal specialties and classic French desserts.
Each creation is crafted with the finest ingredients, ensuring exceptional taste and quality.
The cozy ambiance and friendly staff make it a perfect place to enjoy a relaxing treat.
AIGRE DOUCE is a must-visit for dessert lovers and anyone seeking a sweet indulgence.
